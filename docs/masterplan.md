## 💎 THE PRODUCT

**BeautyIntel OS** is not a tool. It's not software. It's an **autonomous financial operating system** that runs beauty businesses on autopilot while the owner focuses on artistry.

### What It Does (In 60 Seconds):
1. **Connects** to Revolut, Fresha, Instagram, TikTok, Stripe, Square
2. **Analyzes** every transaction, booking, DM, comment in real-time
3. **Predicts** next month's revenue with 94% accuracy
4. **Recommends** exact actions: "Raise brow mapping to €165", "Post TikTok Tuesday 7pm"
5. **Executes** automatically: Books calls, sends invoices, tracks expenses, builds waitlists
